OLD HOUSE FILMS.

A short film production house

Raani

02 MAR 2023 Varun Anto

Raani is a self aware short film that tries to break body stereotypes and shackles placed on people in such casual fashion on a daily level. What if a bus stop represents the entire society's perspective against our main lady, a "fat" woman?

Nadagam

10 March 2021 Varun Anto

In "Nadagam" (The Drama), the protagonist stages a murder drama to confront the perpetrator and make him realize the consequences of his past actions. The film delves into themes of justice, revenge, and confronting the past to seek closure.
